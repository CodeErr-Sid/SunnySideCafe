17

Years Experience

We are doing more than
you expect

Tucked into a pretty little corner of Wolverton is our little haven where it's always sunnysideup, no matter what’s happening outside. Whether you’re in the mood for a steaming cup of coffee, a delightful sandwich, or a milkshake so chocolatey it might just make your day, we’ve got exactly what you need. I got you drooling with the milkshake right? Our menu is packed with goodies designed to satisfy your cravings from AM to the PM. We’re all about using the highest quality, locally sourced ingredients because, well, you’re special to us. Only the best for our favorite people—yes, that’s you! So, pop in, kick back, and let us sprinkle a little extra sunshine into your day. Our doors are always open!

01

We are located in the city center

Sunnyside Up Café is conveniently located in the Heart of Wolverton Milton Keynes,making it the perfect spot for breakfast, brunch, or a cozy coffee break. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, we invite you to stop by and enjoy our warm atmosphere and delicious offerings.

02

Fresh ingredients from organic farms

Wondering what makes our food so delicious? When it comes to ingredients we have personally employed the VIPs of the ingredient world. That’s right, only the finest quality from trusted local sources for our finer customers.

03

Own fast delivery. 30 min Maximum

We live in an era where speed is everything and our delivery service is no stranger to this. Place an order with us, go pick a movie and we’ll be at your door before the opening credits.

04

Professional, experienced chefs

Our chefs are true culinary artists, often described as having the "Midas touch" or as modern-day magicians. While high-quality, handpicked ingredients are essential, they alone can't craft a remarkable meal. With years of experience, our talented culinary team skillfully navigates every challenge to ensure that each dish brings a burst of sunshine and flavor to your plate.

05

Charming, cozy, and refined ambience

We believe that breakfast is more than just a meal—it's an experience. That’s why we’ve thoughtfully crafted our ambiance to transport you to a world of comfort and warmth. From the gentle glow of our lighting to our carefully curated music playlist, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet morning meal for two or a lively breakfast gathering with friends, our space adapts to suit your mood, ensuring that each visit is uniquely memorable.

06

The highest standards of service

Now, let us introduce the true stars of our establishment. Drumroll, please! Just as we infuse a touch of sunshine into everything we do, our service shines just as brightly. Our dedicated team is committed to providing the highest standard of service, ensuring that every moment of your experience is as warm and inviting as the delicious meals we serve.
